About

Hello! I am a software engineer based out of Baton Rouge, LA with expertise in user interface development and design. Currently I work at Fieldwire supporting the iOS application for our construction management software that help teams collaborate and manage their projects more effectively.

Previously, I worked at Apple on the Core WiFi Experience team working on features across the product lineup at the intersection of Wi‑Fi and user experiences. This includes the Wi‑Fi settings UI, features like password sharing, auto joining networks, and more! Additionally, I have had the opportunity to work on features including the new Control Center in iOS, the Precision Finding feature in the Find My app, and many other things.

Outside of my full time job as a software engineer, I am pursuing a Masters degree specializing in Human‑Computer Interaction, work on games and personal apps, and bake bread.
