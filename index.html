About

Hello! I am a software engineer based out of the SF Bay Area. Growing up, I was always fascinated by the technology in personal computers and video games. Later on, this would develop into a need to understand how it things work which lead me to study computer engineering at LSU. As a student, I gained experience through internships in robotics and data analysis. I eventually realized that what I love about technology are the ways it can improve our lives and decided to teach myself iOS app development. This put me on a path to specialize in user interface development and design.

Currently, I work at Apple on the Wi‑Fi Experience team where we work on features across the product lineup at the intersection of Wi‑Fi and user experience. This includes the Wi‑Fi settings UI, features like password sharing, auto joining known networks, and more! In addition to my Wi‑Fi duties, I have had the opportunity to work on features including the new Control Center in iOS, the Precision Finding feature in the Find My app, and many other things.

Outside of my full time job as a software engineer, I am pursuing a Masters degree specializing in Human‑Computer Interaction, work on games and apps for fun, and bake bread.
